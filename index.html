Play Your Best
The Irish Golf Society is a golf society for everyone. We are a rapidly growing community of members from all across Ireland. Founded by brother John and James Herron in 2019, our mission is to bring golfers of all abilities and backgrounds together, on the best courses in the land, to compete for phenomenal prizes!
